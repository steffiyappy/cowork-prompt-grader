How is this graded?

Cowork credit usage scales on four buckets (per the June 2026 Credits Guide): Models — the AI model chosen per task (quality vs speed vs cost); Runtime — cloud orchestration that keeps agents running, including long or chained work; Context — reading the emails, files, meetings and people behind the work; Tools — actions taken (sending, scheduling, posting, creating). Each bucket is rated 0–3 from the prompt, then mapped to the guide's illustrative Light (70–200), Medium (400–600) and Heavy (>1,500) credit bands. Heuristic only — not an exact credit prediction.
